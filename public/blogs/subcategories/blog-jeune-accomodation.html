Temporary accomodation for young people

by Prasanth RAGUPATHY

Résidences Habitat Jeunes welcomes all young people between the ages of 16 and 30: students, interns, jobseekers, younr workers, etc ... for periods ranging from a few weeks to over a year. If you are searching for a temporary accommodation until you find a permanent place, please check with the individual residences. foyers-jeunes-travailleurs

The 4 residences of Toulouse Métropole are listed below.
