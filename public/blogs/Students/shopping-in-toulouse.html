Note:

  • These maps against the shopping places are focused around ISAE SUPAERO. You can find these shops all over the city. Find out the closest one near you.

  • There is no concept of MRP here. All the groceries and vegetables have dynamic pricing.

  • Try to buy them from larger supermarkets like Auchan or ELeclerc.

  • Carrefours have student discouts on Wednesdays. Check with their billing for more information.

Other shopping

1. All the branded outlets like H&M, ZARA, Celio at multiple places in city and in shopping complexes.

2. For cheaper clothings & fast fashion, you can visit PRIMARK and Kiabi.

3. For all the sports related, you can find Decathlon present in multiple places.
