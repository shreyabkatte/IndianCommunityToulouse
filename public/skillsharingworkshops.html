Skill Sharing Workshops


Amid this time of COVID2019, it's the right time to achieve internal happiness if not immortality by sharing the knowledge that you already have. We at ICT plan to arrange a platform for knowledge sharing with the ones who want to learn and improve new skills. If you have some specific technical or soft skills that you want to teach to these interested people then please fill the form below and we will get back to you with the online training organization details.

This kind of training can help improve the skills of the people eventually creating better employment opportunities for them in this unfortunate time where many are on the verge of unemployment and economic loss. Please come forward and join hands in this cause of spreading knowledge.

**This is completely voluntary service and there will be no fee attached. ICT is not an authorized training organization, our objective is primarily to provide a platform for people interested in skill sharing on a voluntary basis.

If you want to share any of your skill to fellow comunity members, then register here.

If you want to attend one of our sessions, then register here.

Since there are limited places available for every session, the registration link might be deactivated. Please write to indiancommunitytoulouse.com for more information
