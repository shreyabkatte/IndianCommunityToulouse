COVID 19 - ICT solidarity

Home COVID 19

During this COVID-19 situation, there are many who have been impacted, emotionally, financially and in many other ways. ICT would like to extend solidarity through various activities.



COVID 19 Community fund:

We are raising funds to support our fellow community, especially students and job seekers who are impacted due to the present situation. We are facing an unprecedented challenge. Any donations will be warmly welcomed as we continue to support people in need.

You can lend your hands by:

  • Monetary donations through HelloAsso


    • For other ways of support, please contact indiancommunity.toulouse@gmail.com

  • e-Gift cards for grocery (carrefour etc..)

  • Share any surplus grocery

Any other ideas are most welcome
