Each one Teach one

This project is inspired from Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam's motivational quote of "Each One Teach One", an appeal for each human being to render their service to the society. If one is unable to dedicate in service towards the society then the minimum one could do is to teach at least one. So let us follow this humble request and make sure you teach one subject/topic to someone else. This initiative is open to everyone from any corner of the world and you can teach any topic of interest like language, technology, cooking, art, music, profession etc.
