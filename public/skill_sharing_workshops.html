Skill Sharing Workshops

Amid this time of COVID 19, it's the right time to achieve internal happiness; if not immortality by sharing the knowledge that you already have. We at ICT plan to arrange a platform for knowledge sharing with the ones who want to learn and improve new skills. If you have some specific technical or soft skills that you want to teach to these interested people then please fill the form below and we will get back to you with the online training organization details.

This kind of training can help improve the skills of the people or introduce them to new topics, eventually creating better employment opportunities for them in this unfortunate time. Please come forward and join hands in this cause of spreading knowledge.

Starting last June, 13 workshops have been conducted, 120+ attendees have been empowered and more workshops are planned for you!

**This is completely voluntary service and there will be no fee attached. ICT is not an authorized training organization, our objective is primarily to provide a platform for people interested in skill sharing on a voluntary basis.

Association ICT

Association Indian Community Toulouse is a non-profit association governed by the French law of 1 July 1901 for associations declared to prefecture. It aims to bring together all the people interested in India & its cultures and represent the Indian community around Toulouse.
